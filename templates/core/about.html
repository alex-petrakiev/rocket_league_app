❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rocket League Hub official?

No, we're a fan-created community platform. We're not affiliated with Psyonix or Epic Games, but we're passionate fans just like you!

What platforms are supported?

Our community welcomes players from all platforms - PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. Cross-platform play means we're all one community!

How do I upload clips?

After creating an account, go to the Clips section and click "Upload Clip". We support MP4, AVI, and MOV files up to 100MB.

Are tournaments free to join?

Yes! All community tournaments are free to join. Some may have prizes sponsored by community members, but participation is always free.
